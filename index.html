Hue Effect in Short-form Video Marketing: 

The Moderating Role of Voice Arousal and Positive Emotion 

ABSTRACT 

Video is one of the fastest growing online advertising offered to consumers, despite extensive research on the image hue, little know how the effect of video hue on short-form video marketing. Based on the video feature, we combine advances in multi-modal machine learning analyzed of video data and focus on the effect of cover page and peak level of hue. We first examine the important role of the cover page hue on click through rate. Then, we test a U-shaped relationship between the peak level of hue and sales performance and then explored the moderation effect of voice arousal and textual valence. Furthermore, our research provides the causality evidence that the effect of hue (cold vs. control vs. warm) on purchase intention and its boundary conditions. These findings make theoretical contributions, and provide practical insights for video creators, advertisers, and short-form video platform managers.
